Popcorn Pops!

Description

Popcorn is a tried and true snack that never disappoints! Make your own with these easy steps!

Ingredients

  • Corn Kernels
  • Oil
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Measure out the kernels.
  2. Add oil to a hot pot with a lid.
  3. Wait for the oil to get hot, then add the kernels, cover and wait for the pops!
