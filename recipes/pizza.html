Pizza Time!

Description

This pizza is made in the artisan style and comes with all the best toppings for a grand old time!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Dried Oregano

Directions

  1. Measure 1 cup of flour and add it to the bowl.
  2. Make a well and add .5 cups of water, yeast and salt. Mix.
  3. Add dried oregano and mix the entire bowl until you have a supple dough.
  4. Cover to proof for 1 hr.
  5. Once risen, roll out and make your pizza!
