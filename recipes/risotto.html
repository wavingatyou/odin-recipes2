Risotto World!

Description

In Risotto World, all you do is stir, stir, stir! This one is a keeper but takes a long time.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Mushrooms
  • White Wine

Directions

  1. Measure out the rice and add it to the pan with 1 cup of white wine.
  2. Add the salt and stir.
  3. Add sliced mushrooms to the pan and allow them to cook for a few minutes.
  4. Stir for several hours until you have a great risotto!
